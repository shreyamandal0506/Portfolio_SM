About Me

Fronted Developer

I’m Shreya Mandal, a passionate Graphic Designer and Front-end Developer with a strong eye for aesthetics and detail. I specialize in creating visually engaging designs and responsive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and tools like Figma and Tailwind CSS. Alongside my technical skills, I have over a year of experience in social media management, helping brands and college communities grow their online presence. Whether it’s crafting a clean user interface, designing impactful visuals, or handling digital campaigns, I love turning ideas into real, meaningful results. I’m always eager to learn, collaborate, and take on creative challenges.
